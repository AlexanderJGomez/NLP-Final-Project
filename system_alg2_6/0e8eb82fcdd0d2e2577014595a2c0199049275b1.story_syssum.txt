Last month in Florida, a school bus driver witnessed a vicious assault on a 13-year-old boy.
Fail to intervene, and it haunts you; intervene, and you may become the victim.
To complicate matters, some violent situations can actually be worsened when a bystander intervenes.
Bus drivers can be trained in the use of tools and techniques that can help disable violent teens (e.g., use of pepper spray).
