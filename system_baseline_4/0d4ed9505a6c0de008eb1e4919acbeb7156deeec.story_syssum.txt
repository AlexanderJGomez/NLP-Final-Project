(CNN)Outside of Israeli politics, Isaac Herzog is not a well-known name.
Throughout the campaign, Herzog has been seen as an underdog, lacking the charisma and the English fluency of Netanyahu.
Herzog says that doesn't bother him at all.
But when Netanyahu called for early elections in 2014, Herzog pegged his bid for the premiership on social reform.
I will change the nature of the division of wealth in a fair and more balanced way, close inequality and give a sense of purpose to the people here in the workplace, in the housing, and in the cost of living," promised Herzog.
