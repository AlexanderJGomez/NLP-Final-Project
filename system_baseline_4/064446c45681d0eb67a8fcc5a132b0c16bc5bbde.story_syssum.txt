Unfortunately, drug murders happen so often in Mexico that they are not news anymore.
Is it possible that these murders grabbed more attention than last month's 50-person massacre because of the connection to social media?
In any case, the messages against online reporting might indicate that social media sites are seen as a threat.
Social media is seen as neutral ground by people in Mexico.
The American media are in a privileged position to report safely on what is happening in Mexico.
Mexican voices present in social media speak of a complex problem with just as much relevance for the U.S. as Mexico.
