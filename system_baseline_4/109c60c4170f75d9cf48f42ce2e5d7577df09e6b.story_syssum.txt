Over 4,400 solar panels compose the roof of Blackfriars bridge in the City of London, making it the largest solar-powered bridge in the world.
On a sunny day, the canopy can produce up to a megawatt of electricity.
"This is the most powerful bridge in the world and it's using solar power in Britain, not a country well known for its sunshine."
