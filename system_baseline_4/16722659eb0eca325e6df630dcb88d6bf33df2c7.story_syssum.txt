The master of macabre, Hideo Nakata didn't start out in film aiming to make horror movies.
He didn't' start out aiming to make horror films, but it was Nikkatsu Studios that Nakata met Hiroshi Takahashi, the screenplay writer for "Ringu."
For Hollywood producers, Nakata has been a gold-mine of remake material.
Nakata then has been credited with turning on a whole new audience to Asian horror films with an altogether more subtle approach to scaring the wits out of people.
He reveals the finer points of his scare tactics to Anjali Rao on Talk Asia this weekend.
