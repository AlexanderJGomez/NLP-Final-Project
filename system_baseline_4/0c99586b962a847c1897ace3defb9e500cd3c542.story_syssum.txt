(CNN) -- Xavi Hernandez has signed an extended deal with Barcelona that will keep him at the Camp Nou for the rest of his career, the Spanish club have announced.
Xavi told Barcelona's official Web site: "I want to hang up my boots here.
"I've been involved with Barca all my life, coming up through the ranks, and I'd like to hang up my boots here.
Club president Joan Laporta said: "It's great news for the club.
From the moment he became one of the captains of the team, with players like Puyol, Iniesta, Valdés, Messi and Piqué, he has shown the personal and sporting profile which proves that the youth academy has triumphed."
The players will always remember him."
