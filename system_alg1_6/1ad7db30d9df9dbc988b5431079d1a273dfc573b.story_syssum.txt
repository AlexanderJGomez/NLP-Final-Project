Many finance professionals often work into the wee hours of the morning.
Now it seems to be spreading.
In addition to damaging the body and mind, long hours at work cut people off from their family and friends.
People who spend most of their waking hours at work or dealing with work-related activities have little time to build and nurture relationships outside of work.
If days are spent only working, then there is little time to put into local clubs, faith groups and associations.
Employees in Germany and France work less hours but get more done.
There are so many good reasons to end the extreme work culture.
We have to make it acceptable to say "no" to an extreme work culture.
