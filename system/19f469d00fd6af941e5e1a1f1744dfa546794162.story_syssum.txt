Many of the girls profiled in the film were helped by these organizations, and the groups continue to work for girls' education in developing countries.
GirlUp -- United Nations Foundation is a campaign inspiring American girls to take action for girls in countries such as Ethiopia, Guatemala and Liberia who face a lack of access to education and are susceptible to child marriage, high maternal death rates and HIV infection.
Plan International USA tries to give girls around the world access to food, water, education, financial security and protection from sexual violence and exploitation.
Room to Read focuses on helping young girls develop literacy skills and a habit of reading.
