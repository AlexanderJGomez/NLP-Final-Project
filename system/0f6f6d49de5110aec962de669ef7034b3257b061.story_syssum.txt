To my readers, I am the author of nine Christian books.
And, to so many over the years who have known my story, I am a man in a race -- a race against illness, against fear, against discrimination.
It has included many twists and turns: from the joy of marrying my high school sweetheart to the sorrow of being rejected by the first church to which I was appointed as pastor.
Instead, the journey affords me a glimpse into the best of what each of us can become.
About real meaning... and about myself: Life is more a mosaic than a measurable frame of joys and sorrows, laughter and tears.
No matter how I try to describe myself, I am more than the sum of what I can say.
