If he doesn't trim two inches off his 42-inch waist soon, the police department in the northern Pakistani state of Punjab could demote or even fire him.
"We saw many of our officers were getting a little big around the waist, so they are bound to do this," said Deputy Police Chief Sheikh Iftikhar.
Pakistan's police officers are often on the front line in the fight against violent extremist groups that have increasingly moved to urban areas and major cities.
His form is far from perfect, and the exercise equipment is old and rusty, but he is determined to shrink his waist and keep his job.
