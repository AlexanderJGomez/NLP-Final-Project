While you still have electricity, read up on some fun games to play with your kids--no lights required!
Hide a stuffed animal and set the kids out on a search for their toy with flashlights.
Create a tent out of sheets and start a story chain or sing songs around a "campfire", either a candle set on a coffee table or a fire place if you have one.
