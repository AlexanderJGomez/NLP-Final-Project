• Kevin Hart and Ice Cube will reprise their roles in the just-announced sequel to Ride Along.
Chris Pasetto will make his directorial debut on the project.
• Haley Joel Osment (The Sixth Sense) has taken on a supporting role in the Entourage movie.
He'll play the part of filmmaker and avid climber David Breashears, who co-directed the 1996 IMAX film Everest and the PBS Frontline documentary Storm Over Everest about the 1996 disaster.
Wendy Moniz (Betrayal) and Trevor St. John (One Life to Live) will play the parents for director Patrick Wang.
