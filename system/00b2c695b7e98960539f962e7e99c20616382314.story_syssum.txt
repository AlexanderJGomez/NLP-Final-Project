Hosted by Yasser Sami and Walid Monam, and produced by their friend Ghazwan Al-Shawi, the show is called "Akou Fad Wahad" -- "There is this guy" -- the typical setup for one of their anecdotes.
"I can't tell you (who) because this means I will give them another chance to come to me again," he laughs.
A typical gag goes like this: "A stoned guy bought two birds for his mother, one tweeting and the other silent.
