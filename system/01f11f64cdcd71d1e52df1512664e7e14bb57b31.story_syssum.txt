(CNN Student News) -- Students will make educated predictions about how many electoral votes each presidential candidate will capture in the 2008 U.S. presidential election.
After students complete their research, have each group analyze its data to determine which candidate it thinks will likely capture the swing state's electoral votes.
Instruct students to create a chart and log their predictions for each U.S. state.
