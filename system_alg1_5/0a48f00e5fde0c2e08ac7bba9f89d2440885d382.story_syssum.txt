Before we permanently lose any other celebrities to their secret hideouts, it's time to consider a way to fight back against the paparazzi.
In fact, it's staring all of us in the face.

"They are baiting you," Baldwin said in his farewell-to-the-world.
Go about your daily life, but have a trusted and well-paid photographer at your side, documenting just how rude, invasive, and aggressive the celebrity chasers can be.
All of this might sound extreme.
It may be too late for Baldwin, but not for other celebrities.
