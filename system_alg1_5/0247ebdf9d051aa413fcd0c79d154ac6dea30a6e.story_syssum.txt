It is a society of potential, innovation, and opportunity.
Youth all around the world have faced unemployment, high tuition fees, and tough economic situations for years.
These frustrations, coupled with difficult economic times, have created an unstable environment and resulted in the launch of initiatives to engage youth as active citizens in political, economic, and social decision-making.
When it hit Jordan it was, by coincidence, around the same time of the Arab Spring.
Unemployment is a major issue in the Middle East and North Africa region, especially in Jordan.
Therefore, many look for jobs abroad, particularly in the Gulf region.
We should collaborate with individuals from our region by crossing borders, creating partnerships, and exploring ideas.
Three years ago, the Arab Spring signified hope.
Therefore, I advise my fellow Arab to prepare, strategize, and implement with utmost passion, wisdom, and honesty.
