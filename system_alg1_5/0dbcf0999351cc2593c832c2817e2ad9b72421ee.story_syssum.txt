John Atta Mills, left, of the opposition NDC has defeated Nana Akufo-Addo, right, of the ruling NPP in the runoff.
Mills, 64, will replace John Agyekum Kufuor as Ghana's president on January 7.
Ghana, a country about the size of England, is the world's second-biggest cocoa and gold producer.
The world recession, however, has hindered growth.
