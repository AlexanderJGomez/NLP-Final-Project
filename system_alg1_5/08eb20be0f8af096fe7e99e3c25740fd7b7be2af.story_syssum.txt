HAVANA, Cuba (CNN)  -- New Orleans, Louisiana, Mayor Ray Nagin arrived in Cuba late Friday on a mission to learn about how to deal with storms, a spokeswoman said.
Since Katrina, New Orleans has adjusted its disaster response with new evacuation plans.
Some aspects of the communist nation's response, including mandatory evacuations, may not be possible in a democracy.
The mayor of Galveston, Texas, another city hit hard by Gulf of Mexico hurricanes, visited Cuba this year.
