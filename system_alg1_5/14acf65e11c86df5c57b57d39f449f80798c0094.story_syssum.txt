Manchester United's Park Ji-Sung in action for his club.
When it comes to Asian football, there's no bigger star than Park Ji-Sung.
But Ferguson has had other ideas, picking Park for some of United's most important games.
Following last season's English Premier League title, and a start in this year's Champions League final, he led South Korea to the 2010 World Cup finals, scoring a late goal against Iran to secure their automatic qualification for South Africa.
To confirm his status, he has just signed a two-year extension at United.
