Apple, too, has outlined its app review guidelines in extensive detail, but has not publicly commented on the CTIA app rating issue.
In its App Store, Apple provides an age rating ranging from 4+ to 17+, and lists the reasons for assigning its maturity rating.
CTIA announced it would be developing its app rating system in March.
Apps would receive their rating almost instantaneously, and if a developer disagreed with the assigned rating, it could appeal it.
