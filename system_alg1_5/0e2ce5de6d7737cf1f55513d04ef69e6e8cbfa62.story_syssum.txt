Cameras recorded the confrontation between Katzenbach, then serving as deputy attorney general, and Wallace.
Katzenbach died of natural causes Tuesday at his home in Skillman, New Jersey, according to Princeton University, where Katzenbach earned a degree and served as trustee emeritus.
Katzenbach, a New Jersey native, attended top prep schools and Ivy League colleges.
