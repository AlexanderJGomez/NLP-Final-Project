San Francisco, California (CNET)  -- On Wednesday, Sony unveiled Move, its motion-sensitive controller.
Among the types of games Move will work with include those that involve swords, bows and arrows, guns, punching and much more.
Further, the controller comes with its own accessory, known as a sub-controller, which will accentuate what's possible with Move.
