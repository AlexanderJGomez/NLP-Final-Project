The 139th Annual Westminster Kennel Club Dog Show took place February 16-17, 2015.
The dogs compete for Best in Breed, then move up to Best in Group, then compete for the grand prize of Best in Show.
Forty-seven breed varieties have won Best in Show awards, as of 2015.
