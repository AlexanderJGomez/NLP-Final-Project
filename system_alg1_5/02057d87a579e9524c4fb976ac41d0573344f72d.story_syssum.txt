Some 106 people tested positive for HIV, according to the National AIDS authority.
"I call for a thorough investigation into the issue," Hun Sen said in a televised speech.
New HIV infections have dropped by 67% from 3500 in 2005 to 1300 in 2013, according to UNAIDS.
The government will allocate US$3.7 million of national funding to HIV treatment from 2015 to 2017.
