Italian authorities have arrested 15 people on suspicion of murdering the Christians at sea, police in Palermo, Sicily, said.
The 12 who died were from Nigeria and Ghana, police said.
Since 2000, according to the International Organization for Migration, almost 22,000 people have died fleeing across the Mediterranean.
CNN's Hada Messia reported from Rome, and CNN's Livia Borghese reported from Augusta, Sicily.
