Tokyo is a huge and often confusing city for travelers, but with these tips you needn't be daunted by the Japanese capital.
Ideally, have a map of your destination, or at least an address written in Japanese.
ATMs Although Tokyo is ultra-modern, you may have to revert to carrying travelers' checks for the duration of your stay.
Metropolis and Tokyo Notice Board are two of the best -- and they're both free.
- If you're eating with other people, don't pour your own drink.
- It's a faux pas be seen eating in public -- especially on a train.
