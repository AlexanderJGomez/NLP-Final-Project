At 23 years old, Stockwell was deployed to Iraq in March 2004.
"Think of the loudest noise you can imagine and our vehicle starts to swerve," she recalls.
"It sounds strange, but as soon as I was told I didn't have my leg, I knew it was going to be OK," the veteran says.
Four months after her injury, she went skiing in Colorado.
"Basically you fit amputees with artificial limbs," she says.
"I dreamed of going to the Olympics when I was younger," she says.
She felt like her life had come full circle: "It made me feel so, so alive."
First place, for the third consecutive year, went to her coach and mentor Melissa Stockwell.
