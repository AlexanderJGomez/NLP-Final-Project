(CNN) -- Are immigrants to the U.S. more likely than the general American population to adopt tablet computers?
In all, 13% reported owning a tablet device, representing about 5 million people in the U.S.
In its study, this device category includes e-readers like Amazon's Kindle.
This might make immigrants more willing to adopt new mobile technology sooner.
