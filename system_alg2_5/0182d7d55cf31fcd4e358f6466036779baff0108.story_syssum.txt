Around $2.7 billion worth of fruit and vegetables were shipped from the EU to Russia in 2013, the bloc's single biggest export to the market.
European countries sold $1.6 billion worth of pork and $1.3 billion worth of cheese and curd to Russia.
The U.S. shipped $1.3 billion worth of food to Russia, with chicken and other poultry making up a quarter of the total value, around $310 million.
In 2013, EU countries sold nearly $1 billion worth of spirits and $733 million worth of wine to Russia.
