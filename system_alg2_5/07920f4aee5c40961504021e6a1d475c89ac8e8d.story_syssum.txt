In the other five-nation pools, England are the top seed in Group G and will have to get past 2008 co-hosts Switzerland, the unpredictable but talented Bulgaria, Wales and Montenegro.
Three-time champions Germany were drawn in Group A with Turkey, who they beat in the 2008 semifinals before losing to Spain.
Bosnia-Herzegovina, beaten by Portugal in the World Cup playoffs last year, were also drawn in Group D.
Group G: England, Switzerland, Bulgaria, Wales, Montenegro.
Group I: Spain, Czech Republic, Scotland, Lithuania, Liechtenstein.
