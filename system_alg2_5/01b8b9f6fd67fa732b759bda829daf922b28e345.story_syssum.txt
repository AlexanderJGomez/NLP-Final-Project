Baghdad (CNN) -- Violence across Iraq has claimed nine lives in the past two days, officials with the nation's interior ministry said Monday.
On Sunday, an Iraqi servicemember and a firefighter died when they responded to the scene of a roadside bombing.
When the servicemember and firefighter responded, another roadside bomb exploded.
One of al-Nujaifi's bodyguards was wounded, the interior ministry officials said.
Others were wounded in attacks, interior ministry officials said.
However, assassinations, gunfire and bombs remain regular occurrences.
