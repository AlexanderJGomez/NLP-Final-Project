"He's going to be part of the history," Addahoumi said about his son, Sammi, who is in the rebel hub of Benghazi.
Over the years, Addahoumi said he has returned to Libya from time to time and that he wishes he could be there now.
"I wish I could be part of this history," he said.
He said he has spent time off and on in Libya since 2005.
