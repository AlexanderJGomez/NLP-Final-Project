Amsterdam Airport Schiphol (CNN) -- It was impossible not to be deeply moved by Harun Calehr when he and his family joined Chris Cuomo live on "New Day" on Wednesday.
We may have sat across from the self-appointed Prime Minister of his self-declared Republic and asked him if he and his men, so many of whom are from Russia, had shot down the plane.
But standing in front of a family who lost two of their children, ten year old Miguel and nineteen year old Shaka Calehr.
Looking at their mother, far too emotional to even speak.
As journalists we are often fickle and fleeting in our gaze, but we remain proud and committed to impartiality.
