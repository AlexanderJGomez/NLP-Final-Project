(CNN) -- Lego boss Jorgen Vig Knudstorp, who pulled the iconic toy brand back into profit, says Europe needs to face up to its harsh economic reality.
Knudstorp says Lego went too deeply into debt, as Europe has also done.
At Lego, Knudstorp will be focused on ensuring the company adapts to the changing economic conditions, which he believes are crucial to ensure survival.
But Knudstorp also emphasis the needs to keep things simple.
CNN's Emily Smith has contributed to this report.
