I started my career as a gender specialist working in many countries where "honor" murders and other acts of violence against women often found considerable public support.
I have seen dehumanizing living conditions breed dehumanized people.
Every day I see Pakistanis robbed of their dignity, their hope, and ultimately their sense of significance.
Today, the feudal elite is entrenched in Pakistan's political system.
For the feudal elite to eradicate poverty and ignorance, they must conjure the political will to create equality by pushing for land reform.
