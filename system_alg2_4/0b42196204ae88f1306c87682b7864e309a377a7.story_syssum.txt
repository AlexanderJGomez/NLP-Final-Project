(CNN)Police have arrested four students from Wesleyan University in connection with a drug overdose over the weekend.
Wesleyan University has suspended the students.
According to the Drug Enforcement Administration, most often Molly is not MDMA, but a toxic mixture of lab-created chemicals.
Some of the chemicals have been reported to cause intense, prolonged panic attacks, psychosis and seizures.
CNN's Jean Casarez, Drew Griffin, Nelli Black and Patricia DiCarlo contributed to this report.
