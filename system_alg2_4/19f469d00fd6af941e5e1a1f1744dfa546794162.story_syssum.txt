(CNN) -- CNN Films' "Girl Rising" tells the stories of girls across the globe and the power of education to change the world.
"Girl Rising" is the centerpiece of 10x10, a global campaign to educate and empower girls.
Many of the girls profiled in the film were helped by these organizations, and the groups continue to work for girls' education in developing countries.
