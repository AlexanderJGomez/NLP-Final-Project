The game's creators are partnering with Zynga, maker of such popular social games as "Farmville," "Cityville" and "Words With Friends," to produce Zynga Slingo.
The Zynga Slingo version allows for playing with your Facebook friends in a variety of ways.
The fourth-quarter report shows the company's games were played by 54 million users daily, an increase from 48 million during the same time in 2010.
