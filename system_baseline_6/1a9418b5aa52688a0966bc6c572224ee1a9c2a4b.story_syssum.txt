How important is your reputation?
Search Yelp reviews and see what people who have already dined at a place have to say about it.
Even though these reputation systems have been created for the specific company -- imagine how powerful it would be to have your reputation in one place.
Reputation will become an increasingly visible part of our everyday transactions.
We may even see reputation system APIs developed to enable the transfer of your reputation to new platforms.
This is an excerpt of a report on reputation currencies, written by Heather Schlegel and commissioned by the Institute of Customer Experience.
