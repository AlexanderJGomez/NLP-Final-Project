The weather here is spectacular.
But the president (and the country) must listen closely to the trends coming from here, because what starts in California often ends up spreading across the country.
There is a state budget deficit which would make even the eurozone blush.
Over the past few years, California has made deep cuts in education, social services, and even releasing some prisoners early to save money.
California is as much a victim of this as any European country.
Austerity California is a glimpse of the future for America; the rest of the country, blinded by extravagant election promises, just doesn't yet realize it.
