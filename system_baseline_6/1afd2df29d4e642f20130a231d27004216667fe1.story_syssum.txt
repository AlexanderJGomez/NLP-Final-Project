Rome (CNN)Muslims who were among migrants trying to get from Libya to Italy in a boat this week threw 12 fellow passengers overboard -- killing them -- because the 12 were Christians, Italian police said Thursday.
Thousands of people each year make the dangerous sea journey from North Africa to Europe's Mediterranean coast, often aboard vessels poorly equipped for the trip.
CNN's Hada Messia reported from Rome, and CNN's Livia Borghese reported from Augusta, Sicily.
CNN's Ben Wedeman contributed to this report.
