Super Bowl XLVIII started out bad for the Denver Broncos.
Denver didn't score until the third quarter, when the game was out of reach.
Manning was thwarted in his quest for a second Super Bowl ring.
Just before the third quarter ended, Denver got onto the scoreboard when Manning threw a 14-yard pass to Demaryius Thomas.
Richard Sherman, the Seattle defense player who mocked San Francisco after winning the NFC championship, was never really challenged.
A man was shot several times during a Super Bowl party at a residence in Denver, police said.
