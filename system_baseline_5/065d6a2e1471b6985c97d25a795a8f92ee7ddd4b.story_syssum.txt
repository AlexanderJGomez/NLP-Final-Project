The game's creators are partnering with Zynga, maker of such popular social games as "Farmville," "Cityville" and "Words With Friends," to produce Zynga Slingo.
"Slingo" is played by more than 55 million people through online, mobile and casino games.
" 'Slingo' is one of the earliest and most cherished games of the Internet," said Rich Sawel, product manager for Zynga Slingo.
The company also had the top five most-played games on Facebook.
