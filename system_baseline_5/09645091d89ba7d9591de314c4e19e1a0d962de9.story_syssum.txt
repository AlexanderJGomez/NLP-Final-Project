The 139th Annual Westminster Kennel Club Dog Show took place February 16-17, 2015.
Non-invited dogs can also compete if they have won a certain number of major dog show awards.
Seven dogs have won more than one Best in Show.
1876  - The Westminster Kennel Club decides to put on an annual dog show to improve breeds of dogs and to raise public interest.
1907 - The show presents its first Best in Show award, to a smooth fox terrier named Ch.
1984 - The biggest dog to win Best in Show is Seward's Blackbeard, a 155-pound Newfoundland.
This is the 14th time that a wire fox terrier has won Best in Show, more than any other breed.
2015 - Miss P, a 15-inch beagle, wins Best in Show.
