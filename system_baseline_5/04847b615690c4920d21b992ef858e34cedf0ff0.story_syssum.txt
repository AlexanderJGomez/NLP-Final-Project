At age 16, Rob McCullough walked into an LA Boxing gym for the first time.
"I dealt with bullying growing up as a kid because I was always the new guy at the school," he says.
From a childhood with such challenges, McCullough found both a passion and an occupation.
Through the anti-bullying campaign, McCullough said he is sending a message to kids.
"We are guys that fight for a living, and we could obviously be mistaken for bullies, but we are nothing of the sort," McCullough says.
