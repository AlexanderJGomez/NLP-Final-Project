Authorities seized more than $2 million worth of products and were looking at bank accounts to determine the size of the enterprise.
"Health and beauty products like lip balms, oils, shampoos and inhalers are highly regulated in order to protect consumers, but these defendants are charged with going around those protections and stealing the brands of major corporations that comply with the law," District Attorney Kathleen Rice said in the statement.
The case came to light when Valley Stream Fire Department officials in January conducted a building inspection at the site of a fire a year earlier.
The brothers are accused of selling the products to distributors who, in turn, delivered them to retailers in New York, Pennsylvania and Florida.
Counterfeits will not have the same quality or consistency of real products.
