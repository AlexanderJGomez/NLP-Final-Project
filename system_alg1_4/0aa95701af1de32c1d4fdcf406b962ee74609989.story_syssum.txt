(The Hollywood Reporter)It's official: AMC's "The Walking Dead" companion series has a title.
AMC confirmed that the companion series is set in Los Angeles but revealed nothing more beyond that it will focus on new characters and storylines.
Cliff Curtis ("Gang Related") stars as Sean Cabrera, a teacher who shares a son with his ex-wife.
"Fear The Walking Dead" will premiere in the late summer with season two set for 2016.
