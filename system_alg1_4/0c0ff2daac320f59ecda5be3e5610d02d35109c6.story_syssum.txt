At a news conference in Quetta, Kakar said that in Balochistan, the flooding and rain killed six people in Khuzdar district, four in Turbat and five in Lasbella.
Monsoon rains and flash flooding continue to bring misery to people living in Khuzdar, Jhal Magsi and Loralai districts.
Kakar says the Pakistan army is helping people affected by the floods.
Flood waters are receding in Karachi, Pakistan's largest city, where eight people were reported killed.
Additional deaths were reported in Punjab, Fata and Azad Jammu Kashmir provinces.
